The Kesar story

Memory, season and spice—served with restraint.

Kesar begins with flavours we know by heart: smoke from the tandoor, the perfume of saffron, the patience of a dum and the generosity of an Indian table.

We refine those memories without losing their soul. The result is expressive, modern food grounded in regional technique and honest ingredients.
